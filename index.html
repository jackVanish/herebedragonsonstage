On the deck of a ship stands ‘The Atlantean,’ a single-seat submersible designed to withstand the deepest reaches of Earth’s oceans. Next to it stands Levin, whose dream of a research mission to the bottom of the ocean has become a reality in spite of questions surrounding his scientific views. The deeper he goes, and the darker it gets, the more dire his dive becomes, and the surer he gets that he is being stalked by something just out of sight…

A story of wonder, fear, and romance, Here Be Dragons tracks a scientist’s descent into a world where he will either shed light on one of the world’s last great mysteries, or become a part of it.
